June 2024 · 2 minute read

‘National Treasure: Edge of History’ features a stellar cast, including Catherine Zeta-Jones. See more photos of the spinoff in our gallery.

The world of National Treasure is expanding on Disney+. National Treasure: Edge of History is currently airing on the streaming service. The series follows Jess Valenzuela and her friends. Jess’ life is turned on its head when she’s given a clue to a centuries-old treasure that might be connected to her long-dead father. Jess has a knack for solving puzzles, and her skills are put to the test as she and her friends follow a series of clues hidden in American artifacts and landmarks.

The show features a great ensemble cast, including Lisette Olivera as Jess, Catherine Zeta-Jones as Billie, Jake Austin Walker as Liam, Jordan Rodrigues as Ethan, Zuri Reed as Tasha, Antonio Cipriano as Oren, and Lyndon Smith as Agent Ross. Justin Bartha and Harvey Keitel have also reprised their roles from the National Treasure franchise. See more photos of the National Treasure spinoff in our gallery now.
