Super Mario Bros. Wonder is $20 off at QVC during Black Friday

January 2024 · 1 minute read

Super Mario Bros. Wonder is a pretty awesome game, but it’s such a new (and popular) game that it has been resistant to price drops. But for Black Friday, there’s a rare promotion that could save you twenty bucks!

At QVC, first-time shoppers can take $20 off their first orders of $40 or more with code HOLIDAY20.

As we wrote in our review, Super Mario Bros. Wonder embodies everything great about the Nintendo Switch. And it borrows from popular FromSoft games in unexpected ways without becoming too difficult.
