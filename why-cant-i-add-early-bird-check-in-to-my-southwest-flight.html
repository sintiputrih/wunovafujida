Why can’t I add early bird check-in to my Southwest flight?

Southwest Airlines offers a convenient early bird check-in option that allows passengers to secure a better boarding position by automatically checking them in and assigning them a boarding number ahead of general check-in. However, there may be certain instances where this option is not available or cannot be added to a flight reservation. Here are a few possible reasons why you might not be able to add early bird check-in to your Southwest flight:

One possible reason is that early bird check-in may have already reached its capacity for the particular flight you are trying to add it to. Due to the popularity of this service, there might be limited availability, especially if you are attempting to add it close to your departure date. It is advisable to add early bird check-in as soon as possible after purchasing your ticket to avoid disappointment.

Another reason could be that you might have already missed the deadline to add early bird check-in. The option is typically available for purchase up to 36 hours before your scheduled departure time. If you are trying to add it within this time frame, you may no longer be able to do so.

There might also be certain restrictions or limitations based on the type of fare you have purchased. Early bird check-in is usually not available for certain types of tickets, such as group reservations or bookings made through a travel agency. It is important to review the terms and conditions of your ticket to determine if this option is applicable.

Additionally, if you have already checked in for your flight, whether online or at the airport, you will not be able to add early bird check-in. Once you have received a boarding position, it cannot be changed or upgraded. Early bird check-in must be added prior to completing the check-in process.

It is worth noting that early bird check-in is an optional service provided by Southwest Airlines and comes with an additional fee. Therefore, if you have concerns about the cost or availability, you may opt to secure a favorable boarding position by checking in online exactly 24 hours prior to your flight’s departure time.

Frequently Asked Questions (FAQs) about Southwest’s early bird check-in:

1. Can I add early bird check-in to my Southwest flight after purchasing my ticket?

Yes, you can add early bird check-in to your Southwest flight even after purchasing your ticket. However, it is recommended to add it as soon as possible to secure a better boarding position.

2. How much does early bird check-in cost?

The cost of early bird check-in varies, but it is typically around $15-25 per person, per flight segment. The exact price can be confirmed during the booking or check-in process.

3. Can I add early bird check-in to a group reservation?

No, early bird check-in is not available for group reservations. Each member of a group reservation must check in individually to receive a boarding position.

4. Can I add early bird check-in if I booked my flight through a travel agency?

In most cases, early bird check-in is only available for flights booked directly through Southwest Airlines. If you booked your flight through a travel agency or third-party website, you may not be able to add this option.

5. Can I add early bird check-in for my connecting flights?

Yes, early bird check-in can be added to connecting flights. However, it is important to note that early bird check-in is valid for the individual flight segments and not for the entire itinerary.

6. Is early bird check-in refundable?

No, early bird check-in fees are non-refundable. Once purchased, the fee will not be refunded even if you later decide to change or cancel your flight.

7. Can I add early bird check-in if I have already checked in for my flight?

No, once you have completed the check-in process, either online or at the airport, you cannot add or modify your boarding position, including early bird check-in.

8. Can I add early bird check-in if I have a mobile boarding pass?

Yes, you can still add early bird check-in even if you have a mobile boarding pass. The boarding position will be assigned to you automatically, and you can still use your mobile boarding pass for convenience during the boarding process.

9. Can I add early bird check-in for my return flight only?

Yes, early bird check-in can be added to individual flight segments. If you wish to add it for your return flight only, you have the option to do so.

10. Can I transfer my early bird check-in to someone else?

No, early bird check-in is non-transferable. The purchased service is specific to the individual named on the reservation and cannot be transferred to another passenger.

11. Can I add early bird check-in after the 36-hour deadline?

No, early bird check-in cannot be added within 36 hours of your scheduled departure time. It is crucial to add this option before the deadline to ensure availability.

12. Can I add early bird check-in if I have a companion pass?

Yes, early bird check-in can be added even if you have a companion pass. However, it is important to note that the early bird check-in fee will be applicable for each passenger, including the companion.
