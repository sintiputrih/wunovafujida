What does auto mean on slow cooker?

AUTO: the auto setting will first cook on a high temperature and then reduce automatically to a lower heat setting. This setting is ideal for slow cooking meals overnight or when you are away for most of the day.

How long can you leave a slow cooker on auto?

Most cookers automatically shut off after 20 hours. Experts say you should not leave food in the slow cooker for extended hours. In general, leave the food on warm for 2 to 4 hours maximum. After this, your food is ready to be transferred to the refrigerator.

Do crock pots need water in the bottom?

A crockpot is a sealed cooking appliance. It cooks food for about 4-10 hours on low heat, barely hitting a boiling temperature. During the cooking process, no steam is released, so there is little to no water lost. In most cases, you do not need to put water in a crockpot.
