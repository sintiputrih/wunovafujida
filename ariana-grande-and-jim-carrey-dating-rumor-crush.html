Was Ariana Grande And Jim Carrey's Insane Dating Rumor Based In Any Ounce Of Truth?

August 2024 · 4 minute read

During the early-2000s, Ariana Grande rose to fame due to her role in the beloved family sitcom Victorious and its spin-off series Sam & Cat. After both of those shows came to an end, Grande went on to launch her singing career and become one of her generation’s biggest stars.

Thanks to the lovable way she rose to fame and how incredibly talented she is as a singer, Ariana Grande has millions of extremely loyal fans. As is the case with most people who have that many followers, there has been a lot of interest in Grande’s personal life over the years.

Even though Ariana Grande currently is married to Dalton Gomez and her fans want to know all about their relationship and wedding, there still is a lot of interest in her famous exes. Of course, it could easily be argued that people talk about when Grande dated Pete Davidson the most.

On top of people still talking about Ariana Grande’s former relationship with Pete Davidson, there is a lot of interest in the fact that she is rumored to have dated a comedy legend. Putting aside the rumors, it is interesting to get down to the facts about whether Grande really did date Jim Carrey or not.

Ariana Grande’s Long Lasting Connection To Jim Carrey

For a long time, stars who went on the talk show circuit would take part in one interview after another in which they would be asked the same questions about their latest project over and over. More recently, that has begun to change thanks to shows like YouTube’s Hot Ones mixing up the format.

Related: Ariana Grande Completely Embarrassed An Interviewer During A Live Radio Show

Even though there is value in interviewers asking stars questions about their latest projects, it often is a lot more interesting when they inquire about more timeless topics. A perfect example of that was one of the questions Ariana Grande was asked when she appeared on Live! With Kelly and Michael in 2015.

During the aforementioned interview, Michael Strahan asked Ariana Grande who her childhood crush was. In response, Grande revealed that she has always had a crush on Jim Carrey and she continued to feel the same way about him at the time of the interview.

"My childhood crush, like, my lifelong crush, it kind of all goes together -- it's Jim Carrey. I love him so much.” After revealing how much affection she has for Carrey, Ariana Grande went on to describe what it was like to meet him for the first time.

"I met him, and like, cried. It was really weird. It was so weird. It was so uncharacteristic of me. I'm usually sort of chill. I'm usually somewhat contained, but I met him and [makes crying face]. I couldn’t control myself”

“I was kind of nervous to meet him, because, I was like, you know, he's been my crush forever. What if he's not as nice as I'm expecting? He was so over-the-top nice, and perfect, and just as I hoped he'd be.”

“[He said to me in] his classic Jim Carrey, 'Nice to meet ya. Thank you. Remember, be nice.' I was like, 'Oh my God, you're so cute. I love you.' I was like, 'Wow, help me God, thank you.'"

Around the same time as the aforementioned interview, Ariana Grande posted a video of herself meeting Jim Carrey for the first time on Instagram. Thanks to that video, it is clear that Grande’s description of that meeting was accurate since she was clearly overcome with emotion.

Did Ariana Grande And Jim Carrey Ever Date?

After Ariana Grande revealed her lifelong crush on Jim Carrey, she went on to make a cameo appearance on the actor’s show Kidding. After filming the cameo, photos of Grande and Carrey looking friendly on the set of the show were posted online.

On top of working with Jim Carrey on the show Kidding, Ariana Grande has quoted the famous actor’s comments about mental health issues on social media in the past. If all of that wasn’t enough, Grande even got a tattoo of the catchphrase Jim Carrey’s Truman Show character was known for on her shoulder.

Related: Here's Why Ariana Grande Appeared On Jim Carrey's Show 'Kidding'

For his part, Jim Carrey has responded to Ariana Grande in the past. Most notably, when Grande quoted Carrey’s take on mental health issues, he commented with nothing but praise for her. “I admire your openness. I wish you freedom and peace. I feel blessed to have such a gifted admirer.”

Based on the mutual admiration that Ariana Grande and Jim Carrey obviously seem to share, rumors that the pair were a couple began to pop up. Despite that, there has never been any evidence that Grande and Carrey have ever been anything aside from friendly with each other.
