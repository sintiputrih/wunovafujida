Career

Minogue got her big break in 1986 when she was cast in the soap opera “Neighbors.” She caught the attention of the music industry’s bigwigs, and in 1987 she acquired a recording contract with Mushroom Records, an Australian label.

The massive success of ‘The Loco-Motion‘ in Australia in 1988 prompted British recording artists Stock, Aitken, and Waterman to sign her to their record labels in the hopes of turning her into an international sensation.

In 1989, Kylie released a new album called “Enjoy Yourself,” which she co-wrote with Jason Donovan, her “Neighbors” co-star and boyfriend at the time. The duets from this album went straight to the top of the charts.

Kylie’s popularity grew to the point where she was cast in the film “The Delinquents” in 1990.

In 2001, she released her eighth studio album, Fever. Across Australia, Europe, and the United States, the record sold 8 million copies. The single “Can’t Get You Out of My Head” became her most successful single.

Kylie’s studio album, “Aphrodite,” was released in 2010. Stuart Price executive produced the album with Kylie, Jake Shears, Calvin Harris, Nerina Pallot, Tim Rice-Oxley, Pascal Gabriel, Lucas Secon, and Kish Mauve.

Charity

Minogue has been active in philanthropy in addition to her musical and acting careers. She contributed to a recording of “Do They Know It’s Christmas?” in 1989 to raise money for Ethiopian anti-famine initiatives.

She helped raise money for Haiti earthquake relief in the 2010s, and in 2020, she donated $500,000 to help with firefighting efforts in Australia due to bushfires.

Minogue’s other charitable endeavors have included assisting mistreated children, financing AIDS research, and raising funds and awareness for cancer research.

Great Ormond Street Hospital

Every year, the Great Ormond Street Hospital in London, England, treats over 100,000 children with the most complicated, unusual, and frequently fatal illnesses.

Government support and, most significantly, endowments obtained by the hospital’s charity, the Great Ormond Street Hospital Youngsters’ Charity, ensure that these children receive the best medical treatment possible.

From 2010 onwards, the GOSHCC expects to raise over £20 million in contributions to augment the government’s National Health Service grants.

Since 2010, Kylie Minogue has strongly supported the hospital and has participated in several promotional and fundraising events organized by GOSHCC.

Breast Cancer Campaign

The Breast Cancer Campaign was founded to encourage scientific excellence in breast cancer research, especially improved diagnosis and treatment and timely intervention to cure or prevent the disease from progressing.

Kylie Minogue has been a regular participant in the Breast Cancer Campaign’s many summits and fund-raising activities since 2011, after recovering from her personal five-year battle with breast cancer.

Prevention of Cruelty to Children

The National Society for the Prevention of Cruelty to Children is a child-care organization located in the United Kingdom that has pioneered work in helping children recover from various forms of abuse and neglect.

The NSPCC is the only children’s charity with legislative powers granted by Queen Victoria in 1895 through a royal charter, allowing it to protect children at risk of abuse.

Apart from considerable grants from the UK government, the group also receives thousands of pounds in donations from child rights advocates and celebrities such as Kylie Minogue.

The Mcgrath Foundation

After Jane McGrath’s diagnosis and treatment for breast cancer, the McGrath Foundation was co-founded by great cricketer Glen McGrath and his late wife, Jane McGrath.

Following Jane’s initial recovery from breast cancer, the couple was asked to write a book on their experiences with the disease, which became “A Love for Life,” the earnings used to establish the McGrath Foundation.

The foundation’s main goal is to provide national funding for breast care nurses, as three-quarters of all Australian women with breast cancer have limited or no access to this type of care.

For more than five years, Kylie Minogue has been a vocal supporter of the causes.
