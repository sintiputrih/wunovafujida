About Bachelor in Paradise 2023

The ninth season of Bachelor in Paradise, hosted by Jesse Palmer, premiered on September 28, 2023, featuring filming in Sayulita, Vallarta-Nayarit, Mexico. Wells Adams continued his role as the bartender for the sixth consecutive season.

The show’s usual Monday and Tuesday timeslot was shifted to Thursday due to the 2023 Writers Guild of America strike, following the first season of The Golden Bachelor. This season also saw guest appearances from former Bachelorettes Hannah Brown, Katie Thurston, and Charity Lawson, adding a dynamic element to the popular reality dating series.
