G Herbo Wife Taina Williams: Dating History And Children

July 2024 · 3 minute read

G Herbo wife: Taina Williams and one of the popular rappers, Herbo had been in a relationship for over four years, and they share two adorable children.

Herbert Randall Wright III, better known by his stage name G Herbo (formerly Lil Herb), is a rapper from Chicago, Illinois.

He signed with the Chicago record label Machine Entertainment Group when he was 16 years old in 2011.

Wright independently released several mixtapes, including Welcome to Fazoland (2014), Pistol P Project (2014), Ballin Like I’m Kobe (2015), and Strictly 4 My Fans (2016). His debut studio album, Humble Beast, came out in 2017.

This was followed by Swervo (2018), a collaborative album with producer Southside and Wright’s first major label release with Epic Records.

His third studio album, PTSD (2020), reached the top ten on the Billboard 200 chart, as did his next two albums, 25 (2021) and Survivor’s Remorse (2022). 25 was Wright’s first project released via Republic Records.
