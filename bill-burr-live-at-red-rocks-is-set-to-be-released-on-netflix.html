Bill Burr: Live at Red Rocks Is Set To Be Released On Netflix

March 2024 · 3 minute read

Bill Burr: Live at Red Rocks, Netflix’s most recent special, is slated to be released on the streaming service. Bill Burr himself made the announcement, taking to Instagram to share the news and the special’s premiere date. He posted on Instagram, saying:

“It’s a whole new hour of stupidity, demonstrating how little I’ve evolved over the years.”

Fans are naturally eager to hear more about his upcoming special. Here’s all you need to know about Bill Burr: At Red Rocks, live.

Who exactly is Bill Burr? What exactly is Bill Burr: Live at Red Rocks about?

William Frederick Burr, better known as Bill Burr, is a stand-up comedian, actor, and writer from the United States. Why Do I Do This? (2008), Let It Go (2010), You People Are All the Same (2012), I’m Sorry You Feel That Way (2014), Walk Your Way Out (2017), and Paper Tiger (2019), which garnered a nomination for Best Comedy Album at the 63rd Annual Grammy Awards, are among his most memorable stand-ups.

Burr, in addition to doing stand-up comedy, conceived, co-wrote, and voiced the main character in the Netflix animated sitcom F Is for Family (2015-2021). He has also played Patrick Kuby on AMC’s Breaking Bad (2011-2013), Migs Mayfield on Disney+’s The Mandalorian (2019-present), and Ray Bishop in the film The King of Staten Island (2020). Bill Burr Presents: Friends Who Kill, a recent Netflix special, was likewise organized and presented by him. His most recent work is Bill Burr: Live at Red Rocks, which is available on Netflix. The program, directed by Mike Binder, was shot in Colorado’s mountain desert. It’s Burr’s seventh hour-long Netflix special. View the trailer below:

Bill Burr discusses his ongoing journey to be a better person in the program. He gives an inspiring speech on women and feminism, as well as how taking m*shrooms has improved his life. He also talks about how he discovered common ground with homosexuals and how his five-year-old daughter helped him deal with his rage problems. Burr also discusses cancel culture and how he handles negative feedback from his wife. In addition, he revisits a life-changing insight he had while doing a heated stand-up act.

When will Bill Burr: Live at Red Rocks be broadcast? What is the best way to view the comedy special?

Bill Burr: Live at Red Rocks will be available on Netflix worldwide on July 12, 2022, at 3 a.m. ET. Because it is a Netflix special, the stand-up will only be accessible on the Netflix streaming site.

To see the episode, fans must first join the streaming service. Netflix provides a variety of plans from which its customers may choose. Bill Burr: Live at Red Rocks captures him in his element, blending humor from his personal experiences.
