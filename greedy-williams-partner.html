Who Is Greedy Williams Partner?: Greedy Williams, officially known as Andraez Montrell Williams is an American football player.

He developed a passion for playing football at a younger age, has been consistent throughout his career, and has become one of the most sought-after football players.

At this time of writing (Sunday, August 19, 2023), Williams is a football cornerback who is a free agent.

He played college football at LSU and was drafted by the Cleveland Browns in the second round of the 2019 NFL Draft.

On October 12, 2020, Williams suffered a nerve injury in his shoulder during training camp and was placed on injured reserve.

After recovering, he had a hamstring injury on September 9, 2022, and was again placed on injured reserve. He was activated a month later (October 15)

On Thursday, March 16, 2023, Williams was signed by the Philadelphia Eagles on a one-year contract but could not climb up the depth chart this summer.

In August 2023, he hit the headlines after he was released by the Philadelphia Eagles on August 19. Williams played the past three seasons with the Cleveland Browns, starting 21 games.
