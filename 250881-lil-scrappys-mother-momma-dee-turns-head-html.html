Lil Scrappy's Mother Momma Dee Turns Heads with Her Cleavage on Display in a Black Crop Top

April 2024 · 3 minute read

Lil Scrappy's beautiful mother, Momma Dee, shared a picture of herself in a daring black outfit. She is living proof that women of all ages can pull off a stunning look.

Deborah Bryant, who goes by the name Momma Dee, is not your average woman. The timeless beauty who is mother to rapper Lil Scrappy defies the standards of women's clothing.

The 55-year-old doting mother took to Instagram to share a snap of herself in a bold look. Some people saw it as risky clothing, but others found it adventurous.

A DARING LOOK

Momma Dee exuded confidence in an all-black ensemble. She wore a long-sleeved crop top with an untied string in front. The style of her top showed her ample cleavage.

She paired it with black leggings which highlighted her toned legs. Beside her ravishing snap, the "Love & Hip Hop Atlanta" reality star captioned her post:

"All Queens Can Feel Sexy At Any Age‼️"

The reality star's look left many fans speechless. Some fans praised Momma Dee for keeping her beauty and body in check through the years.

A fan took to the comment section and expressed that she looked stunning in her outfit and asked for Momma Dee's advice on how to look gorgeous as her. Another fan noted:

"?? you look beautiful momma."

TAKING MOTHERHOOD SERIOUSLY

Momma Dee has come a long way in being the breadwinner of her family. Lil Scrappy revealed in a past interview with MTV that his mother did everything in her power to raise him.

The Bryant matriarch, who comes off as an overprotective mother, sold drugs and pimped girls in the past. She knew that it was illegal, but she persevered to make ends meet.

CORRECTING HER SON'S BEHAVIOR

Lil Scrappy became witness to all of his mother's hardships. Despite his mother's illicit business, the "Money In the Bank" hitmaker didn't have any resentment towards her.

Despite her dark past, Momma Dee didn't fall short as a parent. After Lil Scrappy ranted online about the transgender community, Momma Dee immediately slammed his son for displaying such behavior.

Momma Dee shared on her Instagram feed that while she loves Lil Scrappy as her son, she disagreed with the "No Problem" singer's statement about the community. She apologized to them on behalf of her son.

The rapper, who shares daughter Xylo and son Breland with wife Bambi Benson, had a similar criticism in 2015. When Caitlyn Jenner transitioned, Lil Scrappy said that it was "a racist situation."
