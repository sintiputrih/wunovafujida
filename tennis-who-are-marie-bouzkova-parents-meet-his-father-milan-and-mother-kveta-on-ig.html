Meet Czech Tennis Player Marie Bouzková Parents – Father Milan And Mother Květa

Marie Bouzková was born into a family of tennis players. Though her parents never pursued the sport professionally, they played it as a hobby on the side.

Father Milan Bouzková and mother Květa Bouzková were ecstatic when Marie showed promising signs as a tennis player. The two were ardent tennis fans and thought their daughter could pursue the sport professionally.

Although Marie was born in Prague, she relocated to Florida when she was ten years old, first training at Bollietieri Academy for two years, then working with her father before joining Requeni in 2014.

Starting at the tennis club her parents owned, she has gone on to play in Grand Slam Tournaments, US Open, Wimbledon, Wuhan Open, and more.

She has always looked up to Pete Sampras’ as a tennis idol. Like all tennis players, she admires Serena Williams and Rafael Nadal.

She speaks English, Spanish, and a little German and Czech and is currently pursuing a business administration degree at Indiana University East.
