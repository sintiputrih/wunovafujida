Sam and Citra have had a rollercoaster of a relationship on 90 Day Fiance, and fans think the relationship may already be going one step further, as fans think she’s pregnant.

The 90 Day Fiance couple first met on a dating app after Citra was bored at work. However, as Citra was in Indonesia, and Sam was in the US, it took them a whole two years to meet. Despite all the bumps in the road, the two are still going strong, and it’s reported they’re now married. Some fans even think there may be a TLC baby on the way.

90 Day Fiance fans think Citra is pregnant as they watch confessionals

New couple Sam Wilson and Citra were first introduced to us on screens at the end of 2023. Although the exact date is unclear, we can assume the series was filmed a few months prior. Again, it is unclear when exactly the confessionals are filmed, but we can assume it’s a similar time to the scenes.

A big part of the couple’s relationship storyline was the barriers due to religion. The 26-year-old comes from a strict Muslim background and had to explain to Sam how she must avoid physical intimacy before marriage.

Wilson grew up in a completely different background and the Missouri native found his partner’s beliefs difficult to understand. However, he later agreed to ‘convert’ for the wedding, and his partner.

Despite this, it doesn’t stop some 90 Day Fiance fans on X, formerly known as Twitter speculating Citra is pregnant.

“Citra already looks pregnant!” exclaimed one, whilst another responded: “Was just thinking the same thing.”

According to reports, the couple wed in September. Even if the confessionals were filmed after this, it’s unlikely the star would be showing. We’re putting it down to rumors for now, but who knows what will happen in the future.
