Biography

William Valdés was born in Havana, Cuba on January 10, 1994. Most well known for playing Ora Santisteban in the first season of the Nickelodeon Latin America series Grachi, he is also recognized for his work in episodes of the American-produced Spanish language soap operas El Rostro de la Venganza and Voltea Pa’Que Te Enamores. He was featured along with Chino & Nacho in the artist Estephy’s 2013 “Dime Si Tú” music video. During his childhood years, he trained at Cuba’s National Circus School.
