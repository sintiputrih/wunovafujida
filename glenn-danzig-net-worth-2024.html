Early Life and Career Beginnings

Glenn Danzig was born on June 23, 1955, in Lodi, New Jersey. He developed an interest in music at a young age and was particularly influenced by Elvis Presley and Black Sabbath. In the mid-1970s, Danzig formed the punk rock band Misfits, which gained a cult following for its unique blend of punk and horror themes.

Formation of Samhain and Danzig

After the disbandment of Misfits in 1983, Danzig formed Samhain, a band that incorporated elements of gothic rock and heavy metal. In 1987, the band evolved into Danzig, with Glenn as the lead vocalist. The band’s self-titled debut album, released in 1988, was a commercial success and established Danzig as a prominent figure in the heavy metal scene.
