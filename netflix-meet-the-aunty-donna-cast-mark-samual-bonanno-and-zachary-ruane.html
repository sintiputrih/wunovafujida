Netflix: Meet the Aunty Donna cast Mark Samual Bonanno and Zachary Ruane! Tue 17 November 2

March 2024 · 3 minute read

Aunty Donna’s Big Ol’ House of Fun is a brand new Netflix release in 2020, so let’s meet the show’s cast.

Mark Samual Bonanno, Zachary Ruane and Broden Kelly make up the ‘faces’ of comedy group Aunty Donna. Sam Lingham, Max Miller and Tom Armstrong are also members of the team.

Aunty Donna’s Big Ol’ House of Fun series 1 is comprised of six episodes. The show is made up of hilarious comedy sketches which often feature celebrity cameos. It’s safe to say the series is pretty unique as the opening scene of series 1 begins with a song “Everything’s A Drum“.

Aunty Donna: Mark Samual Bonanno

The first member of Aunty Donna to get acquainted with is actor and writer Mark Samual Bonanno.

He studied acting at University of Ballarat Arts Academy and previous to Aunty Donna’s Big Ol’ House of Fun, he was best known for The Wizards of Aus, TV Man and Unclespace.

According to IMDb, Aunty Donna’s Big Ol’ House of Fun is an “absurdist adventure through their everyday lives.”

Mark has over 10,000 followers on Instagram. And by the looks of things, his IG account as almost as absurd as it gets as its made up of the same image (of him and his dad crouched next to a car) with different edits. The first upload was made in September 2019, so Mark’s essentially been uploading the same photo for over a year now. See the most recent version on his IG account.

Speaking to The Guardian in 2020, Mark said: “Some of us are a lot more politically minded in the group than others, but we just prefer to live in the absurd,” says Bonanno. “It’s the way our brains work.“

Meet Zachary Ruane

Next up, 30-year-old Zachary Ruane is another member of Aunty Donna.

Find the Australian actor and writer on Instagram @zacharyruane where he has over 17.5K followers.

As well as being a part of Aunty Donna, Zachary co-hosts a podcast – ‘Mish and Zach’s Leguizamarama!’.

Who is Broden Kelly?

Aunty Donna’s Big Ol’ House of Fun viewers will also meet Broden Kelly during series 1.

Broden and the rest of the Aunty Donna team hail from Melbourne, Australia and met at University of Ballarat Arts Academy.

The team has seen huge success since they launched, now boasting 337K subscribers on YouTube and a Netflix series!

Broden has over 20K followers on IG, follow him @brodenkellyismyname.
