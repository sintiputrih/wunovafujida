Jessica Simpson's 4-Year-Old Daughter Maxwell Gets &quot;Grounded&quot; in New Instagram

July 2024 · 1 minute read

The apple definitely doesn't fall far from the tree in this family.

Jessica Simpson took to Instagram over the weekend to share a beautiful snap of her and husband Eric Johnson's daughter Maxwell Drew Johnson, and she looks more like her famous mom than ever before.

In the photo, the adorable 4-year-old wears a ruffled navy blue one-piece swimsuit covered in tiny white polka dots as she lays in the wet sand. The little girl's gorgeous blonde hair is fanned out around her face as she smiles and relaxes in the sunshine. Simpson took the opportunity to joke about her daughter being grounded—both literally and figuratively—in the picture's caption, writing: "'I be grounded y'all' #maxidrew."

A few weeks ago, the mother of two revealed on the social media platform that Maxi had cut her own hair right before the first day of school following a 'gram that she posted earlier this summer which highlighted the tyke's super shiny strands.

It looks like this star has her hands full.
