Jenna Vulcano is an American comedienne and actress best known for being the sister of American comedian and Impractical Jokers star Sal Vulcano.

Jenna Vulcano was born on 4 August 1986, in Staten Island in New York, the USA to parents, Sal Vulcano Sr. and Dianne Fernadez-Vulcano. However, her parents are currently divorced.

Jenna Vulcano was raised alongside two siblings, Dana Vulcano and Salvatore Edward Vulcano. Her brother Salvatore Edward is a well-renowned comedian, producer, and actor. There is no information regarding her early life, educational background and academic qualifications.

Jenna Vulcano is presently working as a medical receptionist in New York, Staten Island area. However, she used to work as a coordinator and a receptionist. She rose to prominence following her debut appearance on Impractical Jokers in 2012 in an episode called “Strip High Five”.

Jenna Vulcano Daughter

Jenna Vulcano does not have a daughter at the moment. However, she once posted on Twitter that she has a daughter named Leah which turned out as a joke.

Jenna Vulcano Spouse

Jenna Vulcano is currently not married. She previously got married to American American author and producer, James Murray of Impractical Jokers in 2014. The marriage ceremony which was was attended by family and friends was actually a prank, Jenna performed on her brother Sal Vulcano.

Jenna Vulcano and Murray got divorced the night after the honeymoon.
