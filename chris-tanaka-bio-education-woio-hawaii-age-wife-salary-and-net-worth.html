Chris Tanaka Biography

Chris Tanaka is an American journalist, news reporter, and news anchor. He anchors the 5 p.m. and 11 p.m. news on WOIO Cleveland 19 News as well as the 9 p.m. and 10 p.m. on CLE43. Cleveland 19 News is a CBS-affiliated television station that is based in Cleveland, Ohio in the United States of America. Before joining WOIO, Chris worked as an anchor and reporter for Hawaii News Now.

Chris Tanaka Early Life

Tanaka is half Japanese. He was born in Taipei, Taiwan and raised in Amman, Jordan.

Chris Tanaka Education

Tanaka attended the Boston College between 1993 – 1997 where he was awarded a degree in Bachelor of Arts (BA) in Communication and Media Studies. Additionally, he also attended Regis University located in Denver, Colorado. Here he studied and graduated with honors in Master of Business Administration in 2011.

Tanaka the news reporter joined the Cleveland 19 news team in July of 2016. This was triggered by his five years of staying in Honolulu, Hawaii in the United States of America.
