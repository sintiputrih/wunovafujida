Shefali Jariwala was born on December 15, 1982, and as of the date of this writing, she is 38 years old. She was born in India’s Gujarat state of Ahmedabad. She was also born a Sagittarius and identifies as a Hindu.

How did Shefali Jariwala start her Professional Career?

Shefali Jariwala made her acting debut in the “Kaanta Laga” music video in 2002. Following the release of the music video, Shefali experienced an overnight phenomenon due to the song’s success. She adopted the monikers “The Thong Girl” and “Kaanta Laga Girl.” Shefali also participated in the dancing reality competition Boogie Woogie on Sony TV in 2008.

Her other music videos include “Maal Bhari Aahe – DJ Hot Remix,” “Kabhi Aar Kabhi Paar Remix,” and others. She made a fleeting appearance in the 2004 movie “Mujhse Shaadi Karoge.” The dancer appeared in the music video for “Pyar Humaayn Kis Mod Pe Le Aaya” by Meet Bros in 2006. She also made an appearance in the song “Naa Board Irada Bus Pankaja” from the movie “Hudugaru.”

Shefali gained notoriety in 2012 as a result of her appearance on the dance reality series “Nach Baliye 5” on Star Plus. She also had a starring role in the web series “Baby Come Naa.” After a protracted break, she made a comeback in 2019 for the 13th season of Big Boss. She was said to be Saumya Tandon’s replacement in “Bhabhiji Ghar Par Hain” But the rumors have been refuted by the producers.
