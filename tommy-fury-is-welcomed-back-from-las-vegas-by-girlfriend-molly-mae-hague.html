Tommy Fury is welcomed back from Las Vegas by girlfriend Molly-Mae Hague

August 2024 · 5 minute read

A delighted Tommy Fury was welcomed back from Las Vegas by his girlfriend Molly-Mae Hague on Wednesday who decorated their home with a Halloween display.

The boxer, 22, was in Sin City over the weekend watching his brother Tyson Fury demolish Deontay Wilder in a thrilling heavyweight contest.

But now he is back with Love Island star and influencer Molly-Mae, 22, who gave him a spooky surprise with the seasonal display at their spacious Manchester abode.
