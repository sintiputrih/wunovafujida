Niecy Nash Bio (Wiki)

July 2024 · 4 minute read

Who is Niecy Nash?

Niecy Denise Betts, popularly known as Niecy Nash is a well-recognized American comedian, actress, and television host, best known for her performances on television.

Nash is best known for Reno 911! (2003), Claws(2017), and Guess Who (2005).

Moreover, she has also earned the two Primetime Emmy Awards for Outstanding Supporting Actress in a Comedy Series from the series Reno 911! from 2003- 2009.

How old is Niecy Nash?

Niecy Nash was born on 23 February 1970. At present, her age is 51 years old. Likewise, her birthplace is Palmdale, California, the USA.

Niecy is the daughter of her father, Sonny Ensley whereas her mother is Margaret Endley. Moreover, she had a younger brother as well, Michael Ensley who died in 1993 after he was shot and killed on the high school campus.

Talking about her nationality, Niecy Nash holds an American identity whereas her ethnicity is African-American.

Regarding education, the actress took her graduation degree from California State University, Dominquez Hills in Carson, California, in Drama.

Niecy Nash- Professional Life, Careers

Professionally, Niecy Nash debuted in the 1995 movie, Boys on the Slide. Also, she made a guest appearance in “NYPD Blue”, “Judging Amy”, “Reba”, “Girlfriends”, “CSI: Crime Scene Investigation”, and “ER”.

Later she performed in the recurring role as Eveline Walker in the CBS drama series, City of Angels in the year 2000.

Nash stepped forward after portraying the role of Deputy Raineesha Williams and T.T. on the Comedy Central comedy series, “Reno 911!” in 2003. She pursued the role for six years until 2009.

Moreover, Niecy Nash also earned high popularity for hosting the Style Network’s show, Clean House from the year 2003 till 2010 for more than 7 years.

Apart from this, the comedian actress has also provided the voice of Mrs. Boots on the ABC Family animated series, “Slacker Cats” and featured as Rhonda, alongside Jerry O’Connell , in the short-lived Fox sitcom, Do Not Disturb in the year 2008.

Nash also emerged on the tenth season of ABC’s Dancing with the Stars that started in March 2010.

Freshly, Niecy Nash starred in Ava DuVernay’s Netflix Limited drama series, “When They See Us”.

In 2020, the talented actress featured Sylvia in the drama film, “Uncorked”. Also, she has portrayed many roles in series such as Stolen by My Mother: The Kamiyah Mobley Story, A Million Little Things, Mrs. America, and Never Have I Ever.
