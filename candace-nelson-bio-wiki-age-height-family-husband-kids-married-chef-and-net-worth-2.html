    Candace Nelson Pastry Chef

    On April 13, 2005, Candace and her husband opened Sprinkles Cupcakes, one of the world’s first cupcake bakeries. They started in a 600-square-foot (56 m2)-shop in Beverly Hills, California. The store was designed by an architect from Vienna and the logo and packaging were recreated by a former Martha Stewart employee. The business proved to be successful the first week as they sold over 2,000 cupcakes the first week.

    As of 2020, Sprinkles has 24 locations throughout the United States including New York, with plans to open in 4 more cities including London and Tokyo. The Nelsons also started a traveling “Sprinklesmobile”, a Mercedes Sprinter van designed by Sprinkles architect Andrea Lenardin.

    Some of the high-profile clients include  Oprah Winfrey, Blake Lively, Katie Holmes, Ryan Seacrest, Tom Cruise, Barbra Streisand, and Serena Williams.
